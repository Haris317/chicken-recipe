Chicken Malai Boti Recipe

This melt-in-the-mouth, restaurant-style Chicken Malai Boti aka Malai Tikka is
super tender and makes the perfect fuss-free, luscious meal.

Ingredients:

  • 500g boneless chicken, cut into cubes
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 1/2 cup fresh cream
  • 2 tablespoons ginger garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon white pepper powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala powder
  • 1/4 cup grated cheese
  • Salt to taste
  • Wooden skewers, soaked in water

Instructions:

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine yogurt, fresh cream, ginger garlic paste, white pepper powder, cumin powder, garam masala powder, grated cheese, and salt to make the marinade.
  2. Add the chicken cubes to the marinade and mix well. Let it marinate for at least 2 hours in the refrigerator.
  3. Thread the marinated chicken onto the soaked wooden skewers.
  4. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat. Grill the chicken skewers, turning occasionally, until they are cooked through and slightly charred, about 15-20 minutes.
  5. Serve hot with naan or rice.
